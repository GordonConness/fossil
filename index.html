The Plankton Project

Mission Statement:

Over the coming years and decades, the United States will transition away from the fossil fuel industry. Depending on politics and policy, that change could happen drastically in the coming years or could be a slow burn for the next few decades. The United States needs to make this transition, but also needs to realize that many jobs, lives, and communities across the country will be negatively impacted. For a successful transition from fossil fuels to renewable energy, we must identify those who are most at risk to be left behind. The Plankton Project's objective is to help identify the cities, counties, and regions across the country that will suffer economically due to the disappearance of the fossil fuel industry. It is imperative that the carbon-free, renewable energy movement identifies these communities and has a plan in place to make sure the livelihoods of fossil fuel workers do not disappear.
